The Start of a New Beginning and Fresh Perspectives

As we step into this new year, I find myself reflecting on all the possibilities that lie ahead. There's something magical about the beginning of a new chapter, the fresh start that January brings, and the opportunity to set new goals and aspirations. This year, I'm committed to growth, learning, and making meaningful connections.

The transition into this new year has been both exciting and complicated. I've spent time thinking about what truly matters to me like my relationships, my passions, my responsibilities, and the impact I want to make for my family who has always supported me up until now.

December and January Events

The past months gave me moments I will always cherish. On January 10, my beloved Tita Beleng finally got married. All the family efforts paid off, and the celebration was full of joy. I never thought I would cry at a wedding, but I did. It felt special seeing a hopeful future begin through a new memory. We also spent more meaningful time with my auntie. As we grow older, we understand the value of family connection. Even when problems arise, gathering together still matters most. These moments reminded me that shared time is priceless. I carry these lessons with me as I move into the new year.

Embracing Change and Challenges

Change can be intimidating, but I've learned to see it as a chance to grow. This year came with challenges, including changes in our living situation. It wasn't easy, and many family issues surfaced along the way. While the process was difficult, it was necessary for healing and growth.

Despite everything, we stayed connected as a family. Being together, even in small moments, made things feel lighter. We still found reasons to celebrate and smile. In the end, family support made the hardest times more bearable. As I write this first post of the year, I'm filled with optimism and excitement for what's to come. Here's to new beginnings, endless possibilities, and a year filled with growth, joy, and meaningful experiences. Thank you for joining me on this journey!

January 4, 2026 | 12:48 AM

Reflecting on the special reunion with my GR12 SHS teacher, Ms. Zarina Paredes, fills me with warmth and gratitude. She treated me with so many thoughtful gifts—like delicious food, a fun movie outing, a photobooth session, and even emptying her points because I played too many crane games. The highlight was her gifting me a purple Labubu bag, which has been incredibly useful ever since.

Beyond the gifts, she went out of her way to help me navigate back home safely since I was still new to the streets, not wanting me to risk getting lost. We had so much fun together, and I never expected to be treated with such kindness and enjoyment of my company by a teacher. It was truly special.

I'm grateful that despite not meeting for a long time, we still shared that same deep connection that helped me achieve so much during my time at Makati High School. Her support and belief in me made a lasting impact on my journey.
